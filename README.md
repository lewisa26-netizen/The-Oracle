🔮 The All-Knowing Genie
An AI-powered guessing game. Think of any character — real or fictional — and the genie will figure out who it is through a series of yes/no questions.
Powered by Claude AI.
How to play
Just open the link and think of any character. Answer the genie's yes/no questions honestly and it'll guess who you're thinking of — usually within 10–15 questions.
Works with anyone: celebrities, athletes, politicians, fictional heroes, villains, anime characters, historical figures — anyone.
Running locally
You'll need Node.js and an Anthropic API key.

Then open public/index.html in your browser and enter your API key.
